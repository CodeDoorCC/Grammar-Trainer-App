What Uubung is all about...

We know learning German is hard. So we created Uubung!

Uubung helps you practice those areas of German language that require more practice than others, like the noun genders and the case system. You can also use it to practice your vocabularly by CEFRL level (A1, A2, etc) as well as by frequency level.

Vocab by level

Uubung covers levels A1 through B2 based on the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFRL)

Vocab by frequency

Practice German vocabulary gender by order of frequency, from most common words to less common words

Vocab by field

Practice German vocabulary relevant to certain fields, like medicine, law, and tech!

Vocab by category


Practice German vocabulary relevant to shopping, home, animals, colors, and more!
